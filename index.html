Frequently Asked Questions

How can I get involved with InspireNext?

There are several ways to get involved with InspireNext! You can volunteer your time, become a mentor, partner with us as an organization, or donate to support our programs. Visit our "Get Involved" page to learn more about each opportunity and find the right fit for you.

What age groups do you serve?

Our programs primarily serve youth and young adults between the ages of 14-30. Specific age requirements vary by program, so we encourage you to check individual program pages for detailed information.

Do you offer programs in locations outside your main office?

Yes! We operate programs in multiple cities and communities. Some programs are also available online, making them accessible regardless of location. Check our Programs page for specific location information.

How can my organization partner with InspireNext?

We welcome partnerships with organizations that share our mission! Partnership opportunities include corporate sponsorships, employee volunteering programs, in-kind donations, and program collaborations. Please contact our partnership team at partnerships@inspirenext.org to discuss possibilities.

How quickly can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 1-2 business days. For urgent matters, please call our main office during business hours.
